How to search and display an A.A. meetings in Maharashtra?

To find a AA meeting, perform a search using the search box displayed just above the meeting list table displayed below. You can narrow down your search by using full or partial keyword, you may also use multiple keyword in your search. For e.g
  • To find all meeting in Mumbai on a Tuesday you may type Mumbai Tue
  • To find, meetings in a particular Town, City or District, type the name of the Town, City or District e.g Ratnagiri
  • To find how many AA Groups have the word "Ekta" in their group listing, type Ekta or ekta
  • To find all meetings in Andheri East on a Tuesday you may type Andheri East Tue
  • You may also sort table listing by columns, by clicking the small arrow next to every column title.
